Instructions

  1. Open XD.
  2. Click on the Artboard tool.
  3. Make an artboard sized 728W by 354H.
  4. Click on the Polygon tool.
  5. Draw a triangle sized 149W by 106H.
  6. Make its border color #707070 and its fill color #FFFFFF.
  7. Make its coordinates 83x and 63y.
  8. Make its border size 1.
  9. Have a corner pointing up.
  10. Repeat steps 4-9 but change the coordinates of this second triangle to 538x by 63y and change the size to 150W by 114H.
  11. Repeat steps 4-9 again but change the coordinates of this third triangle to 289x by 201y, and change its size to 160W by 77H.
  12. Click on the line tool.
  13. Draw a horizontal, solid line with size 602W by 4H.
  14. Make its coordinates 71.5x by 100.5y.
  15. Make its border color #31F7B3 and its border size 1.
  16. Repeat steps 12-15 but change its size to 127W by 1H and its coordinates to 308.5x by 225.5y.
  17. Repeat steps 12-15 again but change its size to 96W by 0H, its coordinates to 109.5x by 134.5y, and its border color to #183DDB.
  18. Repeat steps 12-15 but change the size to 99W by 0H, its coordinates to 563.5x by 140.5y.
  19. Repeat steps 12-15 but change the size to 104W by 0H, and its coordinates to 316.5x by 252.5y.
  20. Click on each triangle and line to make sure the appearance/opacity of each is 100%.
  21. Click on File, Export, All Artboards.
  22. Name the file E13_forAlex.
  23. Set the format to PNG, Export for: Web, Designed at 1x, then click Export All Artboards.
  24. Email a PNG attachment of the file to me.
